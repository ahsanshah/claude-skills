## Key Brand Elements

**Primary Colors:**
- Neon Green: #17FF9B (signature color)
- Deep Purple: #4934BA (primary brand)
- Dark Teal: #001820 (text/headers)

**Fonts:**
- Headers: Aptos Display (bold)
- Body: Aptos
- Fallback: Calibri or Arial

**Slide Types:**
- Title slides: Purple gradient background
- Section dividers: Solid purple
- Content: White/light gray background
- Data-heavy: Two-column or full-width layouts

## When to Use This Skill

Use BTPPT when creating:
- ✅ Executive briefings and board decks
- ✅ Customer presentations and demos
- ✅ Internal roadmap and strategy presentations
- ✅ Product launches and feature announcements
- ✅ Any presentation representing Billtrust externally
